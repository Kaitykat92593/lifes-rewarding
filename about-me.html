About Me

Welcome to my blog again. Let me give you a little more about me. I have been married for almost 8 years to my husband. He is from Nepal and we have two little boys. A 5 year old and a 20 month old. I am currently working on learning more about tech and make my career in the intersection of tech and business. I love finding ways to save money so deals are some of my favorite things to figure out. I am always trying to find ways to make life easier and love sharing what I find. I am so excited to share more with you over time and hope that you find something that can help you make life easier too!
