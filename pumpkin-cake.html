Pumpkin Cake

I love to bake! It is one of my favorite things to do. Baking is such a calming pass time for me. It helps me to feel grounded and I love sharing with others. Often bring one or more of these cakes to others so they can have some yummy homemade goods. Growing up baking was so integrated in our household it just seems natural to do the same with my family. Thanksgiving, Christmas, or just throughout the year baking is something that can bring such a homie environment. So, when pumpkin season rolls around this is one of my go to recipes. It is so soft and moist. Let me know what you think in the comments below.

Pumpkin Bundt Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¼ C All-Purpose Flour
  • ¼ C Cake Flour
  • 1 TBSP pumpkin pie spice *or make your own - see recipe notes
  • >1 TSP baking soda
  • ¾ TSP baking powder
  • ½ TSP salt
  • 1 C vegetable oil or canola
  • 1 C white sugar
  • ¾ C brown sugar
  • 2 TSP vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 15 oz can pure pumpkin NOT pumpkin pie filling
  • 1 C Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips and White Chocolate Chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F degrees. Grease and flour 2 6-cup capacity bundt pan greased or 1 12-cup capacity
  2. In a large bowl whisk together the flour, pumpkin pie spice, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate very large bowl beat together the oil, white sugar, brown sugar, vanilla extract, eggs, and pumpkin puree until smooth.
  4. Carefully beat the flour mixture into the pumpkin mixture about ½ at a time.
  5. Put some chocolate chips in the top of the pan
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared bundt pan and smooth the top.
  7. Add more chocolate chips to the top
  8. Put bundt cake(s) in the preheated oven for 35 minutes for 6-cup or 60 to 70 minutes for 12-cup, or until an toothpick comes out clean and the top feels slightly firm to the touch.
  9. Let the cake in the pan for 20-30 minutes. Then carefully insert the pan, to let the cake slide out and continue cooling on a wire rack.

Notes:

  • You can make your own pumpkin pie spice with 2 TBSP cinnamon, ½ TSP ginger powder, ½ TSP ground nutmeg, and ⅛ TSP ground cloves.
  • I usually make it with a big can of pumpkin and double it
  • You can use a 12-cup bundt cake pan instead of doing two 6-cup bundt cake pans

I do not usually put any icing as the chocolate chips add the needed sweetness but if you would rather and icing here is one I would suggest or putting whipped cream on the top. If you do the icing, I would suggest omitting the chocolate chips.

Cream Cheese Glaze

  • 1 TBSP butter softened
  • 3 OZ cream cheese softened
  • 1 C powdered sugar or more as needed
  • 1-2 TBSP milk

Instructions:

  1. In a medium bowl beat together the butter and cream cheese until soft. (Or whisk by hand).
  2. Beat in the powdered sugar followed by the milk.
  3. Beat in a little extra powdered sugar or milk if needed, until the glaze is thin enough to drizzle.
  4. Transfer the cooled cake to a plate, and drizzle the glaze on top.
  5. Slice into pieces and serve.
